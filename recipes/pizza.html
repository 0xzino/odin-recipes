Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings. It's perfect for sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add your desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
