Risotto

Description

Risotto is a creamy Italian rice dish cooked with broth, wine, and flavorful ingredients like mushrooms or seafood. It's a comforting and elegant meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the onion until translucent.
  2. Add the Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the white wine and stir until absorbed.
  4. Gradually add the broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until the rice is creamy and cooked.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
