Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made with long, thin noodles and a savory tomato-based sauce. It's a simple yet satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti noodles and top with Parmesan cheese.
