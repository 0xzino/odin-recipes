Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone, and a dusting of cocoa powder. It's a decadent treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

  1. Dip the ladyfingers in coffee and layer them in a dish.
  2. Whip the heavy cream with sugar until soft peaks form, then fold in mascarpone cheese.
  3. Spread the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with a dusting of cocoa powder.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
